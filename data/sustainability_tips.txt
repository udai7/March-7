=== TRANSPORT TIPS ===

TIP: Switch from petrol car to electric vehicle
CATEGORY: Transport
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 3.4 kg CO2/day (74% reduction for 20km daily)
DIFFICULTY: Hard
DESCRIPTION: Electric vehicles produce significantly lower emissions, especially when charged with renewable energy. Consider the total lifecycle emissions and charging infrastructure availability.

TIP: Use public transportation instead of driving
CATEGORY: Transport
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 3.4 kg CO2/day (74% reduction for 20km daily)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Taking the bus or train dramatically reduces your carbon footprint. Public transport is 3-4 times more efficient than driving alone.

TIP: Carpool with colleagues or neighbors
CATEGORY: Transport
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 3.1 kg CO2/day (67% reduction for 20km daily)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Sharing rides splits emissions among passengers. Carpooling with just one other person cuts your transport emissions in half.

TIP: Cycle for short distances under 5km
CATEGORY: Transport
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 4.6 kg CO2/day (100% reduction)
DIFFICULTY: Medium
DESCRIPTION: Cycling produces zero emissions and improves health. Ideal for urban commutes and short trips.

TIP: Work from home when possible
CATEGORY: Transport
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 4.6 kg CO2/day (100% reduction on remote days)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Remote work eliminates commute emissions entirely. Even 2-3 days per week makes a significant impact.

TIP: Combine errands into single trips
CATEGORY: Transport
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 1.5 kg CO2/day (30% reduction)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Trip chaining reduces total distance driven and cold starts, which are less efficient.

TIP: Maintain proper tire pressure
CATEGORY: Transport
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.3 kg CO2/day (7% reduction)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Properly inflated tires improve fuel efficiency by 3-5%. Check monthly for optimal performance.

TIP: Avoid air travel when alternatives exist
CATEGORY: Transport
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 2.5 kg CO2/day per 100km avoided
DIFFICULTY: Medium
DESCRIPTION: Flying is the most carbon-intensive transport mode. Choose trains or buses for distances under 500km.

=== HOUSEHOLD TIPS ===

TIP: Switch to renewable energy provider
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 3.5 kg CO2/day (100% reduction for electricity)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Many regions offer green energy plans from wind, solar, or hydro sources. Often comparable in price to conventional energy.

TIP: Install solar panels
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 3.5 kg CO2/day (100% reduction for electricity)
DIFFICULTY: Hard
DESCRIPTION: Solar panels generate clean electricity and can pay for themselves over 7-10 years through energy savings.

TIP: Improve home insulation
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 4.9 kg CO2/day (50% reduction in heating)
DIFFICULTY: Hard
DESCRIPTION: Better insulation reduces heating and cooling needs. Focus on attic, walls, and windows for maximum impact.

TIP: Use programmable thermostat
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 2.0 kg CO2/day (20% reduction in heating/cooling)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Automatically adjust temperature when away or sleeping. Can reduce energy use by 10-20%.

TIP: Replace incandescent bulbs with LED
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.3 kg CO2/day (75% reduction in lighting)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: LEDs use 75% less energy and last 25 times longer than incandescent bulbs.

TIP: Air dry clothes instead of using dryer
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 1.2 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Clothes dryers are energy-intensive. Line drying is free and extends clothing life.

TIP: Use cold water for laundry
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.4 kg CO2/day (60% reduction per load)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: 90% of washing machine energy goes to heating water. Modern detergents work well in cold water.

TIP: Unplug devices when not in use
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.5 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Phantom power from standby devices accounts for 5-10% of home electricity use.

TIP: Install low-flow showerheads
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.9 kg CO2/day (50% reduction in water heating)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Reduces hot water use without sacrificing comfort. Saves both water and energy.

TIP: Use energy-efficient appliances
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 1.0 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Medium
DESCRIPTION: ENERGY STAR appliances use 10-50% less energy. Replace old appliances when they fail.

=== FOOD TIPS ===

TIP: Reduce beef consumption
CATEGORY: Food
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 6.3 kg CO2/day (93% reduction if switching to chicken)
DIFFICULTY: Medium
DESCRIPTION: Beef has the highest carbon footprint of any food. Replacing with chicken, fish, or plant proteins dramatically cuts emissions.

TIP: Adopt plant-based meals several days per week
CATEGORY: Food
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 6.3 kg CO2/day (93% reduction per meal)
DIFFICULTY: Medium
DESCRIPTION: Plant-based diets have 50-75% lower carbon footprint than meat-heavy diets. Start with Meatless Mondays.

TIP: Buy local and seasonal produce
CATEGORY: Food
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.8 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Reduces transportation emissions and supports local farmers. Seasonal food is fresher and often cheaper.

TIP: Reduce food waste
CATEGORY: Food
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 1.5 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: One-third of food is wasted globally. Plan meals, store food properly, and compost scraps.

TIP: Choose plant-based milk alternatives
CATEGORY: Food
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 1.4 kg CO2/day (74% reduction)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Oat, soy, and almond milk have much lower emissions than dairy. Oat milk is most sustainable.

TIP: Avoid fast food and processed meals
CATEGORY: Food
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 2.7 kg CO2/day (84% reduction)
DIFFICULTY: Medium
DESCRIPTION: Fast food has high emissions from meat, packaging, and transportation. Home cooking is healthier and greener.

TIP: Grow your own herbs and vegetables
CATEGORY: Food
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.5 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Medium
DESCRIPTION: Home gardens eliminate transportation emissions and packaging. Start with easy herbs like basil and mint.

TIP: Compost organic waste
CATEGORY: Food
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.8 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Composting prevents methane emissions from landfills and creates nutrient-rich soil.

=== LIFESTYLE TIPS ===

TIP: Reduce online shopping and deliveries
CATEGORY: Lifestyle
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 2.1 kg CO2/day per delivery avoided
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Each delivery generates emissions from packaging and transportation. Batch orders or shop locally.

TIP: Buy second-hand and refurbished items
CATEGORY: Lifestyle
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 3.0 kg CO2/day (avoided manufacturing emissions)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Extending product life reduces manufacturing emissions. Thrift stores and online marketplaces offer great options.

TIP: Repair instead of replace
CATEGORY: Lifestyle
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 2.5 kg CO2/day (avoided manufacturing emissions)
DIFFICULTY: Medium
DESCRIPTION: Repairing electronics, clothing, and appliances prevents waste and manufacturing emissions.

TIP: Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers
CATEGORY: Lifestyle
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.4 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Single-use plastics require energy to produce and often end up in landfills or oceans.

TIP: Reduce streaming quality when possible
CATEGORY: Lifestyle
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.2 kg CO2/day (67% reduction)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Streaming in standard definition uses less data and energy than HD or 4K. Audio streaming is even better.

TIP: Support renewable energy projects
CATEGORY: Lifestyle
EMISSION_REDUCTION: Variable (indirect impact)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Invest in or donate to renewable energy projects. Community solar programs make it accessible.

TIP: Minimize paper use and go digital
CATEGORY: Lifestyle
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.3 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Digital documents eliminate paper production emissions. Use e-bills, e-tickets, and digital notes.
