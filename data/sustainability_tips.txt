=== TRANSPORT TIPS ===

TIP: Switch from petrol car to electric vehicle
CATEGORY: Transport
CURRENT_ACTIVITY: Driving petrol car 20km
CURRENT_EMISSION: 4.6 kg CO2/day
ALTERNATIVE_EMISSION: 1.2 kg CO2/day
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 3.4 kg CO2/day (74% reduction)
ANNUAL_SAVINGS: 1241 kg CO2/year
COST: $25,000-$40,000 initial (varies by model)
COST_CATEGORY: high
DIFFICULTY: Hard
TIMEFRAME: Long-term (3-6 months)
PREREQUISITES: Home charging station, access to electricity grid
CO_BENEFITS: Lower fuel costs ($1,500/year savings), reduced maintenance, quieter ride, instant torque
IMPLEMENTATION: Research models, test drive, secure financing, install home charger, apply for government incentives
SOURCE: EPA 2024, IEA Global EV Outlook 2024
CONFIDENCE: High
REGION: Global (varies by electricity mix)
DESCRIPTION: Electric vehicles produce significantly lower emissions, especially when charged with renewable energy. US grid average: 230g CO2/kWh. With 100% renewable charging, emissions drop to 0.2 kg CO2/day. Consider total lifecycle emissions and local charging infrastructure availability.

TIP: Maintain proper tire pressure
CATEGORY: Transport
CURRENT_ACTIVITY: Driving with underinflated tires
CURRENT_EMISSION: 4.6 kg CO2/day
ALTERNATIVE_EMISSION: 4.3 kg CO2/day
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.3 kg CO2/day (7% reduction)
ANNUAL_SAVINGS: 110 kg CO2/year
COST: No cost (DIY) or $5-10 (service station)
COST_CATEGORY: low
DIFFICULTY: Easy
TIMEFRAME: Immediate
PREREQUISITES: Tire pressure gauge, air pump access
CO_BENEFITS: Better fuel economy (3-5%), extended tire life, improved safety
IMPLEMENTATION: Check pressure monthly, inflate to recommended PSI (door jamb sticker), check before long trips
SOURCE: EPA 2024, US Department of Energy
CONFIDENCE: High
REGION: Global
DESCRIPTION: Properly inflated tires improve fuel efficiency by 3-5%. Check monthly for optimal performance. Underinflated tires increase rolling resistance and fuel consumption. Simple maintenance with big impact.

CATEGORY: Transport
CURRENT_ACTIVITY: Domestic flight 500km
CURRENT_EMISSION: 125 kg CO2 (per round trip)
ALTERNATIVE_EMISSION: 8 kg CO2 (train)
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 117 kg CO2 per trip (94% reduction)
ANNUAL_SAVINGS: Variable (depends on frequency)
COST: Often similar or cheaper by train
COST_CATEGORY: medium
DIFFICULTY: Medium
TIMEFRAME: Short-term (requires planning)
PREREQUISITES: Train/bus infrastructure, flexible schedule
CO_BENEFITS: See scenery, work/relax during travel, city center to city center, no airport hassles

=== HOUSEHOLD TIPS ===

TIP: Switch to renewable energy provider
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 3.5 kg CO2/day (100% reduction for electricity)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Many regions offer green energy plans from wind, solar, or hydro sources. Often comparable in price to conventional energy.

TIP: Install solar panels
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 3.5 kg CO2/day (100% reduction for electricity)
DIFFICULTY: Hard
DESCRIPTION: Solar panels generate clean electricity and can pay for themselves over 7-10 years through energy savings.

TIP: Improve home insulation
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 4.9 kg CO2/day (50% reduction in heating)
DIFFICULTY: Hard
DESCRIPTION: Better insulation reduces heating and cooling needs. Focus on attic, walls, and windows for maximum impact.

TIP: Use programmable thermostat
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 2.0 kg CO2/day (20% reduction in heating/cooling)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Automatically adjust temperature when away or sleeping. Can reduce energy use by 10-20%.

TIP: Replace incandescent bulbs with LED
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.3 kg CO2/day (75% reduction in lighting)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: LEDs use 75% less energy and last 25 times longer than incandescent bulbs.

TIP: Air dry clothes instead of using dryer
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 1.2 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Clothes dryers are energy-intensive. Line drying is free and extends clothing life.

TIP: Use cold water for laundry
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.4 kg CO2/day (60% reduction per load)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: 90% of washing machine energy goes to heating water. Modern detergents work well in cold water.

TIP: Unplug devices when not in use
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.5 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Phantom power from standby devices accounts for 5-10% of home electricity use.

TIP: Install low-flow showerheads
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 0.9 kg CO2/day (50% reduction in water heating)
DIFFICULTY: Easy
DESCRIPTION: Reduces hot water use without sacrificing comfort. Saves both water and energy.

TIP: Use energy-efficient appliances
CATEGORY: Household
EMISSION_REDUCTION: 1.0 kg CO2/day
DIFFICULTY: Medium
DESCRIPTION: ENERGY STAR appliances use 10-50% less energy. Replace old appliances when they fail.

TIP: Grow your own herbs and vegetables
CATEGORY: Food
