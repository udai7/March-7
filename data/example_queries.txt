# Example Queries for CO₂ Reduction AI Agent

## Single Activity Queries

1. I drive 20 km daily using a petrol car. How can I reduce my carbon footprint?

2. I eat beef 3 times a week. What are some lower-emission alternatives?

3. My household uses 15 kWh of electricity per day. How can I reduce this?

4. I take a 2-hour flight every month for work. What's the environmental impact?

## Comparison Queries

5. What's better for the environment: driving a petrol car or taking public transport for my 15 km commute?

6. Should I switch from beef to chicken or go fully vegetarian to reduce emissions?

7. Compare the carbon footprint of heating with natural gas versus electric heating.

8. Is it more eco-friendly to buy local produce or imported organic vegetables?

## General Advice Queries

9. What are the top 3 things I can do to reduce my household carbon emissions?

10. How can I make my daily commute more sustainable?

11. What lifestyle changes have the biggest impact on reducing CO₂ emissions?

12. Give me some easy wins for reducing my carbon footprint in the kitchen.

## Category-Specific Queries

### Transport
13. I'm considering buying an electric vehicle. How much CO₂ would I save compared to my current petrol car?

### Household
14. What's the carbon impact of leaving my devices on standby mode all day?

### Food
15. How does eating locally sourced food affect my carbon footprint compared to imported food?

### Lifestyle
16. I want to reduce waste in my home. What are the most effective strategies?

## Dataset Analysis Queries

17. Analyze my weekly activities and tell me where I can make the biggest impact.

18. I've uploaded my monthly activity data. Which activities should I prioritize changing first?
